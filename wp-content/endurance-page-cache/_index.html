ABOUT US

Malens Medcare Limited is a medical diagnostics and biomedical services company with a team of highly educated, technically skilled, friendly and service-driven professionals. Our services are aimed at promoting good health, preventing diseases and ensuring medical conditions are detected early, while also providing the right diagnosis for illnesses for the appropriate treatment to be administered

Using modern and innovative technologies, we operate Automated Medical Laboratory services (Clinical chemistry, Endocrinology, Tumor markers, Drug of Abuse, Hematology, Serology, Immunology, Microbiology, Cytology & Histology), Blood bank services, Biopsy, Endoscopy, DigitalX-ray, Digital Mammography, Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Computerized Tomography (CT-Scan), Ultrasound Scan (2D, 3D/4D), Doppler Studies, Cardiology (Electrocardiogram (ECG) and Electrocardiography) and Spirometry
